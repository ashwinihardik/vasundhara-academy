Facilities Digital ClassroomAt our school, the Digital Classroom is an integral part of our teaching-learning process. In alignment with the progressive framework of the Central Board of Secondary Education and the vision of the National Education Policy 2020, we leverage technology to create an engaging, interactive, and future-ready educational environment.Our classrooms go beyond traditional chalk-and-board methods, incorporating modern digital tools that make learning more visual, immersive, and effective.Objectives of Digital LearningTo enhance conceptual understanding through visual and interactive content To promote active participation and collaborative learning To develop digital literacy and 21st-century skills To support differentiated and personalized learning To make education engaging, relevant, and accessible  Digital InfrastructureSmart ClassroomsEquipped with interactive smart boards, projectors, and audio systems High-quality digital content mapped to CBSE curriculum Ability to display videos, 3D animations, diagrams, and presentations High-Speed ConnectivityReliable internet access across classrooms Seamless access to online educational platforms and resources Learning Management System (LMS)Centralized platform for lesson delivery, assignments, and assessments Students can access notes, homework, and study materials anytime Teachers can upload content, track progress, and provide feedback Device IntegrationUse of tablets/computers (where applicable) Compatibility with digital platforms for blended learning We aim to create a future-ready learning environment where technology empowers both students and teachers. Our Digital Classroom initiative bridges the gap between traditional education and modern learning, preparing students for the digital world.LibraryInformation is at the heart of meaningful learning in schools. To promote a spirit of continuous learning amongst students, Vasundhara assures peaceful and enriching hours in the library. Students are encouraged to cultivate good reading habits and use the library as an important centre for learning. Books, journals and magazines from across the world are made available to the students. Each student can choose his own corner and explore history, literature or science. Undisturbed. Personal supervision is provided to every child to improve his reading and speaking abilities. The library has separate reading halls for primary and secondary sections. Few notable features of our library are:Well stocked libraries for pre-primary, primary and secondary sections.Computerised system to keep libraries up to date.An abundance of reference material including multimedia equipmentNewspapers, magazines, periodicals to keep students &amp; staff well informedReading / story telling sessions with the aid of puppets.
